## 2. Business Summary

Answer in 3-6 sentences:

- What does the company actually sell?
- Which segments matter most?
- What makes returns durable, if anything?
- Which external factors most affect the thesis?

## 3. Key Positives

List the 3-5 strongest reasons the stock could work.
Use evidence, not slogans.

## 4. Key Risks

List the 3-5 most important risks.
Include at least one piece of disconfirming evidence or a reason the market may be right to discount the stock.

## 5. Financial Quality Review

Cover only the metrics that fit the business. Examples:

- Revenue growth and stability
- Margin trend
- Free-cash-flow conversion
- Return on capital or return on equity
- Leverage, liquidity, and refinancing pressure
- Share count changes, buybacks, or dilution
- Dividend quality and payout sustainability

## 6. Valuation Method

State the method chosen and why it fits this business better than the obvious alternatives.
Examples:

- Normalized EPS x justified P/E
- Free cash flow yield with growth and exit multiple
- Book value x justified P/B for financials
- Sum-of-the-parts when a conglomerate structure matters
- Mid-cycle earnings for cyclicals
